Forex Glossary

Key Terms Explained Simply

PIP
Smallest price move. For EURUSD, 1 pip = 0.0001. On a standard lot, 1 pip = $10.
SPREAD
Difference between buy and sell price. Pepperstone from 0.0 pips raw.
LOT SIZE
Standard = 100,000 units. Mini = 10,000. Micro = 1,000. FXPulse uses micro lots.
LEVERAGE
Control more with less. Your 1:30 account means $1 controls $30 in the market.
MARGIN
Deposit broker holds for open trades. Keep margin level above 200% always.
STOP LOSS
Auto-closes losing trade at set price. FXPulse ALWAYS places one — never trades without it.
TAKE PROFIT
Auto-closes winning trade at your target. FXPulse targets 2R (twice the risk).
DRAWDOWN
Peak-to-trough account drop. 10% drawdown from $10K = account at $9K.
BULL / BEAR
Bullish = price going up (buy). Bearish = going down (sell). FXPulse trades both directions.
RENKO
Chart that filters noise — only prints a block when price moves a set amount. FXPulse entry tool.
CONFLUENCE
Multiple signals agreeing at once. FXPulse requires all 5 layers to agree before trading.
R:R RATIO
Risk to Reward. 1:2 means risk $50 to make $100. FXPulse minimum target is always 1:2.
