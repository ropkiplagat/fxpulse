What Happens in a Trade

From Signal to Profit — Automatically

When all 5 layers agree, here is exactly what FXPulse does — without you touching anything:

1

Entry Order Placed

FXPulse opens a BUY or SELL on the currency pair at the optimal price.

2

Stop Loss Set Automatically

A maximum loss limit is placed. If price moves against the trade, it closes here — limiting loss to 1% of your account.

3

Take Profit Set at 2× the Risk

If FXPulse risks 50 pips, the target is 100 pips. You always make more than you risk.

4

Partial Close at 1R

When profit equals the initial risk, FXPulse closes 50% of the position and locks in profit.

5

Stop Loss Moved to Break-Even

The stop loss moves to the entry price. The trade can now only make more money — it cannot lose.

6

Trailing Stop + Final Close at 2R

The stop follows price as it moves in your favour. Remaining 50% closes at the full target.

✅ Once step 5 is complete, FXPulse cannot lose on that trade. Your capital is protected while still reaching for maximum profit.

Forex Glossary

Key Terms in Plain English

PIP
Smallest price movement. For EURUSD, 1 pip = 0.0001. On a standard lot this equals $10.
SPREAD
The gap between buy and sell price. Pepperstone charges from 0.0 pips — among the lowest available.
LOT SIZE
Standard = 100,000 units. Mini = 10,000. Micro = 1,000. FXPulse uses micro lots to keep risk small.
LEVERAGE
Control more with less capital. Your 1:30 account means every $1 you deposit controls $30 in the market.
MARGIN
Deposit your broker holds as security for open trades. Keep your margin level above 200% at all times.
STOP LOSS
Automatically closes a losing trade at a set price. FXPulse ALWAYS places one — it never trades without protection.
TAKE PROFIT
Automatically closes a winning trade at your target. FXPulse targets 2R — twice the amount risked.
DRAWDOWN
How far your account has dropped from its peak. 10% drawdown from $10,000 means account is at $9,000.
BULL / BEAR
Bullish = price going up (FXPulse buys). Bearish = going down (FXPulse sells). The bot profits in both directions.
RENKO
Chart type that filters noise — only prints a new block when price moves a meaningful amount. FXPulse entry signal.
CONFLUENCE
Multiple signals all agreeing at once. FXPulse requires all 5 layers to agree before any trade is placed.
R:R RATIO
Risk-to-Reward. FXPulse always targets 1:2 — risk $50 to make $100. Over 100 trades this compounds powerfully.
